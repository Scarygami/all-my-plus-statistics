Using Google Takeout Data


To get started visit the following URL and click on "Create Archive":
https://www.google.com/takeout/#custom:chat

Once your package is ready you will have to download and extract it. Then you can drag the Hangouts.json file from your file browser and drop it into the field on the left.

All your data will be analysed locally on your computer, nothing will be uploaded. Some public user information about the people you communicate with will be retrieved directly from Google Servers via HTTPS.
